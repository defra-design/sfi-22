The Rural Payments Agency (RPA) is responsible for administering SFI.

16.1 Questions about SFI

If you have any questions about SFI, fill in the query form (which will be published on GOV.UK before applications open) or email us at ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk.

Use ‘Sustainable Farming Incentive’ in the subject header and include your Single Business Identifier (SBI).

You can also call or write.
