Previous rounds of usability testing conducted for SFI were limited in the following ways:

  • 7 x ‘Model Office’ Usability Testing 6th-8th April 2022 – Participants were farmers and land owners working for/associated with RPA creating the risk of bias.
  • 4 x SFI 22 Front End User Testing (Abaco build SitAgri) 21st Feb to 11th March 2022 - Research sessions were led by the researcher as the participant is unable to use the test environment on their own machine. This results in a very unnatural experience for the user and is likely to have an impact on findings.

We now have this prototype that will allow us to conduct usability sessions with customers remotely in order to replicate the SFI application journey as closely as possible.

  • To conduct usability testing of the “Apply for SFI” service
  • To identify and understand any pain points throughout the user journey from awareness through to application submission
  • To better understand how customers use the SFI guidance documents during their application and what information may be required at specific points during the application process
{% endblock %} {% block acc3 %}
  • Users are able to locate the full SFI guidance and will understand that they have to apply via their existing RPA account
  • Users will understand the difference between the two links for SFI in the business overview page and will correctly select the top option as the one they need
  • Users will understand that intermediate action #4 replaces #4 from the introductory level
  • Users will understand the error message they see when indicating they cannot meet the intermediate level action, and will know they can still carry on with introductory levels
  • Users are able to easily select land parcels for introductory and intermediate standard levels using the check box functionality
  • Users are able to easily return to their application once submitted

Application journey:

  1. Apply for SFI
  2. Land details (choose yes)
  3. Management control (choose yes)
  4. Choose standard(s) (choose 'Arable')
  5. Arable introductory levels (yes to all)
  6. Arable intermediate level (choose yes)
  7. Select land parcels (from top to botton, choose introductory, introductory, intermediate)
  8. Check your answers
  9. Submit your application
